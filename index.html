    INTRODUCCIÓN


    The purpose of this website focuses on the collection and identification of sea shells in Colombia and the United States. This project began in May 2024, based on an initial collection of approximately two years. All Colombian specimens in the database were collected by Emmanuel Henao Estrada from various locations, including Barú Island in Cartagena, San Andrés Islas, Isla Palma, and the Camino Verde Hotel in Tolú. On the other hand, the specimens from Florida, in the United States, were purchased from a thrift store in Michigan. All collected specimens were found empty, and no animals were harmed in the process.
